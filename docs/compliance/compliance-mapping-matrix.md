# Compliance Mapping Matrix

## Purpose
This matrix maps applicable Canadian legal and regulatory requirements to Desjardins Insurance policy controls and implementation evidence for the course project.

## Scope Note
This is an academic mapping for project purposes and not an official legal interpretation or regulatory filing.

## Mapping Table
| Requirement Source | Key Requirement | Policy Control Area | NIST CSF Function(s) | ISO/IEC 27001:2022 Alignment (High-Level) | Example Implementation Evidence |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| PIPEDA | Safeguard personal information | Data Protection and Privacy; Access Control | PR, DE, RS | Annex A controls for access control, cryptography, logging, incident management | Encryption settings, access logs, retention rules |
| PIPEDA | Limit collection/use/disclosure to legitimate purposes | Data Protection and Privacy; Risk Management | GV, ID, PR | Information classification, privacy by design, risk treatment | Data classification register, consent language, minimization procedures |
| Quebec Law 25 | Governance and accountability for personal information | Information Security Governance; Roles and Responsibilities | GV, ID | Governance roles, management review, accountability controls | CISO mandate, governance chart, policy approval records |
| Quebec Law 25 | Breach reporting and notification duties | Incident Management; Compliance and Awareness | DE, RS, RC | Incident response, communication and reporting controls | Incident runbook, notification templates, breach log |
| CASL | Consent and unsubscribe obligations in electronic communications | Compliance and Awareness; Legal Review | GV, PR | Compliance controls, communication governance | Consent management process, campaign approval checklist |
| AML/ATF (FINTRAC context) | Identity verification, suspicious activity monitoring and reporting | Risk Management; Logging and Monitoring | ID, DE, RS | Monitoring, event analysis, response workflows | Monitoring rules, case escalation procedure, audit trail |
| OSFI and financial sector guidance | Risk-based security governance and resilience expectations | Information Security Governance; Business Continuity and Disaster Recovery | GV, ID, PR, RC | Risk assessment, business continuity, continual improvement | Risk register, BCP/DR test results, management review minutes |
| Contractual and reinsurer requirements | Security clauses, data residency, control assurance | Third-Party Security; Vendor Management | GV, ID, PR, DE | Supplier security controls, contractual security obligations | Vendor risk assessments, contract clause library, attestation reviews |
| Multi-jurisdiction provincial obligations | Comply with provincial privacy/insurance requirements where clients are served | Compliance and Awareness; Legal; Privacy Officer oversight | GV, ID, RS | Compliance program, legal requirement tracking | Regulatory obligations register, compliance audit plan |

## Control Coverage Summary
- Governance and accountability: Board, CEO, CISO, Privacy Officer oversight
- Data lifecycle protection: classification, encryption, least privilege, retention and disposal
- Operational security: logging, monitoring, incident response, corrective action tracking
- Third-party risk: due diligence, contract controls, ongoing compliance monitoring
- Resilience: backup, disaster recovery, service restoration and post-incident improvement
